Essay Feedback Templater

About

This is designed to assist in the process of organising thought while preparing feedback on an essay. All topics default to "N/A" and will be ignored unless clicked. Click "SUBMIT" to receive a draft text based on these choices that you can then adapt further as needed.

Statement on Data Privacy

This is a web-based, client-side application. We have no information about who is using this, or how. The most we might be able to retrieve (but currently don't) is the basic data (number of page visits, organised by country of origin) processed by Google analytics. Certainly none of the choices or text are sent anywhere.

Statement on AI

This is a simple, deterministic system. There simply isn't any "AI", not in the design/creation and certainly not in any data processing.
